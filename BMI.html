Special Diet Plan as per Your BMI:

Underweight:

  • Focus on nutrient-dense foods to increase calorie intake without relying on empty calories.
  • Incorporate healthy fats from sources like avocados, nuts, seeds, and olive oil.

Normal Weight:

  • Maintain a balanced diet with a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats..
  • Pay attention to portion sizes to avoid overeating..

Overweight:

  • Opt for whole, unprocessed foods and limit intake of sugary, processed, and high-fat foods.
  • Increase consumption of fruits and vegetables for added fiber and nutrients.
